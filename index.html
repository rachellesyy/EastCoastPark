East Coast Park

rediscovering the nation's favourite park



Since its opening in the 1970s, East Coast Park, Singapore's largest park, has been one of the nation's most popular and treasured urban getaways. With a scenic coastline that stretches over 15km and an exciting diversity of sporting, dining and recreational activities offered, it's no wonder.

However, despite attracting more than 7 million people a year, there are still many features of the beautiful park its visitors don't know about.

Think you know all about East Coast Park? Be prepared to be surprised!

Glamping
Allow yourself to immerse in nature without all the discomfort by Glamping! Located in Area G, GlampingCity offers packages ideal an all-inclusive, hassle-free glamping getaway. Check them out at glampingcity.com!
Raintree Cove
The revamped Raintree Cove at Area C, framed by a series of gardens, offers scenic views of the sea, plenty of areas to rest, lounge, or just hang out with friends and family. Perfect for those who prefer a quieter and more tranquil experience at the park!


Cyclist Park
An avid cyclist? Located within Area D of East Coast Park, Cyclist Park is a one-stop hub for cyclists where visitors can practise cycling techniques at two exciting cycling circuits, as well as purchase and repair bicycles.
